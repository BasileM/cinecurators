Cinema Curators creates an unprecedented bridge between contemporary Art & Cinema. Films becomes a new exhibition space and a powerful media support.

Artwork becomes an actor endowed with a unique narrative and interpretative power. Its acquisition through an exceptional sale at the end of the shooting contributes to the financing of the film production and participate to its media coverage.

Cinema Curators provides synergistic production  between artists, galleries, auction houses and producers
